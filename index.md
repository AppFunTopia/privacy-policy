# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 12, 2026
**Developer:** AppFunTopia

## Overview

AppFunTopia apps are designed with privacy in mind. This policy applies to
all apps published by AppFunTopia, including **First Mile : Run Walk Trainer**.

## Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data generated by our apps (such as workout history and session details)
is stored **locally on your device only** and never leaves your device.

## Data Storage

- All app data is stored on your device using local storage
- No data is sent to external servers
- No account or login is required
- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data

## Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising,
or data collection services.

## Permissions

Our apps may request the following permissions solely to provide
core functionality:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Notifications | Display workout status while the app runs in the background |
| Wake Lock | Keep the timer running while your screen is off |

No permission is used to collect or transmit personal data.

## Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any data from anyone,
including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL
with a revised effective date.
