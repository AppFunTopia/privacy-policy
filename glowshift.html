Glowshift — Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

Overview

Glowshift is a free offline arcade game. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data we collect

None. Glowshift does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any server.

Local storage

The app saves your best score locally on your device using Godot's built-in file system. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-party services

Glowshift v1.0 does not include any third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. No account creation or personal information is required to use the app.

Changes to this policy

If future versions of Glowshift introduce features that affect user data (such as ads or leaderboards), this policy will be updated before those features are released.
